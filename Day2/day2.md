# 📚 What I Worked On

Today I learned about **Prompt Engineering** and how writing better prompts improves the quality of AI responses.

I explored:
- What Prompt Engineering is
- Why it is important while using AI tools like Claude
- The difference between a weak prompt and an engineered prompt
- How structured prompts generate clearer, more useful responses
- Designing a LinkedIn-ready AI infographic through prompt engineering

---

# 💡 Key Learnings

- AI performs better when given clear instructions.
- Adding context helps the AI understand the task.
- Being specific produces more accurate results.
- Step-by-step instructions improve response structure.
- Better prompts save time and reduce the need for multiple retries.

# 🎯 Reflection

Today's challenge showed that the quality of AI output depends heavily on the quality of the prompt. Adding context, specifying the audience, and breaking the task into clear steps significantly improves the accuracy, organization, and usefulness of the response.

Prompt Engineering is one of the most valuable skills for working effectively with modern AI tools.
