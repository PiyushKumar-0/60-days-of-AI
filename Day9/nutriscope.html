Educational Disclaimer
NutriScope is an educational, self-tracking tool. All calorie, macronutrient and micronutrient values are approximate estimates derived from generalised food-composition averages, not laboratory analysis of your specific food. Individual nutrient needs vary with health status, pregnancy, medical conditions and lab-confirmed deficiencies. This app does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment, and should never replace guidance from a registered dietitian, physician, or qualified healthcare provider. If any "risk" flag concerns you, please consult a professional before making dietary changes — especially if you are pregnant, managing a chronic condition, or on medication.
All calculations run locally in your browser. No data is uploaded or stored anywhere — closing this tab clears everything.
