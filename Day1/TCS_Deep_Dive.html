Overall View

Strengths

  • Best-in-class capital efficiency: ROE ~52%, ROCE 63%, on a near debt-free balance sheet.
  • Consistently growing free cash flow (₹48,013 Cr in FY26) with strong cash conversion.
  • Trades at a meaningful discount to both its own historical P/E and the IT-services peer median.
  • Stable, unpledged promoter holding and a long dividend-paying track record (77–81% payout).

Watch-points

  • Revenue growth has decelerated to TTM ~5%, the slowest in the trailing decade.
  • FII holding has declined for eight consecutive quarters — a trend worth monitoring alongside FII flows sector-wide.
  • Stock price has underperformed earnings growth (negative 1/3/5-year price CAGR), signalling continued multiple compression risk if growth doesn't reaccelerate.

Key metric to track next

TTM revenue growth and deal TCV (order book) commentary in the upcoming Q1 FY27 earnings call — a re-acceleration here would be the clearest fundamental signal to watch, distinct from any price movement.

Fundamental Quality

Strong — exceptional returns on capital and a conservative balance sheet are only partly offset by slowing top-line growth.
